To list all orgs in your alliance, use
<tab><highlight><symbol>alliance list<end>

To search and add an org:
<tab><highlight><symbol>alliance add 'name'<end>

To add an org using its id:
<tab><highlight><symbol>alliance add 'orgid'<end>

To remove an org using its id:
<tab><highlight><symbol>alliance rem 'orgid'<end>

To update the roster for all orgs in your alliance:
<tab><highlight><symbol>alliance update<end>
